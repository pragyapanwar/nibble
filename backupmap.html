What it is?

IGDTUnistash is an academic portal for the students of igdtuw having an aim to vreate an archivefor all the requirements of students; those that are discovered during the four important years of college.Also we want to make the life of every student of IGDTUW simpler and make these 4 years a much smoother ride.

    Idea Behind UNIstash

    The idea behind IGDTUnistash is to create an archive meant for the students of IGDTUW this archive will comprise of notes of all subjects of various branches,previous year papers,projects done by students,assignments, placement study material etc. As the university is established recently, this archive will comprise of information from the very beginning.

    What's new ??

    Mostly a day or two before the exam seniors get calls and messages from juniors askiing for question papers, notes and lab files. Even if such material is circulated to the junior, not all of them are able to benefit from it. The new thing here is the idea of sharing equally.Also students can take references and even further contribute to the piece of information available in the archive.
